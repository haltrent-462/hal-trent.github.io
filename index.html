§ 01 — Profile

Not a pivot — an extension.

Background

My AI work is a natural extension of fifteen-plus years spent solving the problems AI systems depend on most: how to model, structure, govern, and deliver information so that it is accurate, reusable, and machine-ready at scale.

A Master's in Information Systems from DePaul established the intellectual foundation. The discipline of structured content — schema design, data governance, localization-readiness, multi-channel publishing — is precisely the discipline that separates reliable AI deployments from brittle ones.

I design LLM integrations, RAG-style retrieval and grounding layers, prompt and evaluation frameworks, and automated content-migration tooling. My background spans CTO-level platform strategy, Fortune 500 solutions engineering, and hands-on DITA/XML and CI/CD pipeline development.

Clean, well-modeled, locale-aware content is not a prerequisite most AI engineers think to bring to the table. I do — because I have spent a career building it.

Project № 03 Enterprise Knowledge Consolidation Role Principal Engineer Company Nimble Storage Years 2014 – 2017

Company-wide knowledge migration

I led the company-wide migration of all documentation, API help, support content, and technical marketing into a unified XML cloud CMS — one of the earliest large-scale enterprise knowledge-consolidation projects of its kind.

I designed a custom internal API layer with the CMS vendor, enabling programmatic content access and transformation — early API-first architecture now essential to AI toolchain integration.

I extended DITA-OT with custom plugins generating PDF, web, and LMS outputs from a single source, serving diverse endpoints from a shared knowledge representation. The architecture decisions made then are the same ones AI systems demand now: a single source of truth, programmatically accessible, with output adaptable to consumer context.

Outcomes

  • Unified all enterprise knowledge into a single XML cloud CMS
  • Custom internal API layer enabling programmatic content access
  • DITA-OT plugins producing PDF, web, and LMS outputs from single source
  • Pioneered API-first content architecture now central to AI integration
